triangle_sum
============

Calculate the sum of the maximum of numbers present in each line 


Triangle Sum
By starting at the top of the triangle and moving to adjacent numbers on the row below, the maximum total from top to bottom is 29.

        5
      9  6
    4   6  8
    0  7  1   5

I.e. 5 + 9 + 8 + 7 = 29.
